Privacy Policy

Tuxedo Grid is a cat merging puzzle game designed to be played without accounts, tracking, or data collection.

Last updated: 14 January 2026

Information We Collect

Tuxedo Grid does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Device Data

All game progress (such as best score) is stored locally on your device only (e.g., using iOS local storage / UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Tuxedo Grid does not use third-party services that collect data, such as analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

Tuxedo Grid does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults. The game is designed to be safe and family-friendly.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s features change in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.
